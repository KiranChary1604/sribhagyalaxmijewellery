Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Welcome to Sri Bhagya Laxmi Jewellery ("SBL", "we", "our", or "us"). We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when visiting our website or using our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us personalize and improve your experience. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, physical address, and mobile phone number (collected when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or book a consultation).
  • Appointment Details: Special requests, preferred dates, preferred services, and viewing notes when scheduling a showroom consultation.
  • Transactional Information: Items saved to your showroom selection list, inquiry history, and preferences synced to your profile.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookie identifiers used to facilitate persistent sessions and selection list memory.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • To manage and confirm your private showroom appointments and custom design consultations.
  • To coordinate viewings, sync your showroom selection list across devices, and deliver appointment confirmations via email.
  • To authenticate your identity and administer your SBL account (including managing Elite membership statuses).
  • To send newsletter updates, exhibition alerts, or digital concierge notifications (only if explicitly opted-in).
  • To improve website performance, monitor page health, and secure our system from unauthorized usage.
"At Sri Bhagya Laxmi Jewellery, we believe that trust is the foundation of true luxury. We treat your personal details with the same meticulous care and discretion that we apply to crafting our finest diamond creations."

3. Cookies and Session Storage

Our website utilizes local storage and browser cookies to enable core functionalities. These are used to:

  • Remember items in your showroom selection list even after closing the browser tab.
  • Keep you securely signed in to your customer profile.
  • Provide access control for administrative features, such as our owner dashboard portal.

You can adjust your browser settings to decline cookies; however, some sections of the website (such as the customer dashboard and selection view) may not function correctly as a result.

4. Data Sharing and Protection

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties. We utilize industry-standard encryption, SSL protocols, and secure cloud storage (via Supabase) to prevent unauthorized access, alterations, or disclosures of your customer profile.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your personal data. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal records at any time. To request data deletion or to delete your customer profile entirely, please contact our Digital Concierge at sribhagyalaxmijewellery@gmail.com.

6. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect modifications in our data practices or regulatory updates. Any changes will be published directly on this page with an updated revision date.
